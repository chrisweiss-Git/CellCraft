What this prototype does

This is a clickable prototype of the app we discussed. You can enter a cell configuration, auto-place devices in 2D with reach checks, and play a simplified 3D simulation of the robot servicing a CNC, blow-off, vision check, and good/scrap sort.

Note: This is a browser-only demo with simplified physics & kinematics for speed. The production version will add accurate robot models, collision checks, discrete-event timing, and brand-specific program exports.

Assumed baseline (editable in Wizard)

  • Robot: FANUC M‑20iA (R‑30iB Plus)
  • CNC: HAAS VF‑2 (Discrete I/O)
  • Vision: Cognex In‑Sight 7000 (IO trigger)
  • Part: 6×6×2" steel block (~5 lb)
  • Takt target: 90 s/part
  • Cell area: 12 ft × 16 ft
